Unavailable DMER Case ID Assistance

Options to help the patient obtain their DMER Case ID, which allows medical professionals to access the Driver's Medical Examination Report (DMER) in the portal.

Contact a household member or trusted person
Perhaps there is someone the patient can contact by phone, text or email to help them obtain their DMER Case ID.
Call ICBC
If time permits:
  • Patient can call ICBC and obtain their DMER Case ID over the phone @ ### ### ####.
    NOTE: The patient is required to validate themselves on the phone with ICBC, a medical professional is unable to do this on the patient's behalf.
Call RoadSafetyBC
If time permits: PHONE / CALL BACK WAIT TIMES CAN BE LENGTHY
  • Patient can call RSBC and obtain their DMER Case ID over the phone @ ###-###-####
    NOTE: The patient is required to validate themselves on the phone with RSBC, a medical professional is unable to do this on the patient's behalf.
